Welcome to the Findings Panel Tutorial!
Click on the arrow to the right to continue the tutorial.
Findings Panel
The Findings Panel is the place to see and control trackers on pages you visit.
Controlling Trackers
To block or allow trackers, use the two buttons to the right of each tracker.
Blocking Trackers
Ghostery will block the tracker if the blocking toggle is red and positioned to the right.
Allowing Trackers
Ghostery will allow the tracker if the blocking toggle is blue and positioned to the left. This is the default setting for all trackers.
Making an Exception
Ghostery will always allow the tracker on the current site if the checkmark button is enabled (green).
Pausing Blocking
Ghostery will temporarily stop all blocking if you press the Pause Blocking button. Press the button again to resume blocking.
Whitelisting a Site
Ghostery will allow all trackers on the current site if you press the Whitelist Site button. Press the button again to remove the current site from the whitelist.
Visiting the Options Page
To visit Ghostery's options page, click on the button, and then click the Options button.
